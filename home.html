About Nova Familie

Nova Familie is a surrogacy and IVF coordination company based in the Netherlands. We support individuals and couples who dream of becoming parents, with an approach that is transparent, ethical, and human.

Founded and led by Erik Finch, Nova Familie focuses on clear communication, realistic timelines, and choosing partners who genuinely care about the families they serve.

Our Services

We offer guidance, coordination, and continuous support – not just one clinic referral.

  • Surrogacy coordination in the Netherlands Netherlands
  • IVF support & planning – helping you understand options, timelines, and next steps with partner clinics. Netherlands
  • Cross-border surrogacy & IVF programs Mexico – coming soon Thailand – coming soon
  • Coordination with partner clinics, legal teams, and psychological support professionals.
  • Ongoing check-ins so you always know what is happening and what comes next.

Where We Operate

Netherlands

Nova Familie is based in the Netherlands. We support both local and international intended parents who wish to work within the Dutch framework for surrogacy and IVF.

Our role is to help you understand your options, connect with trusted partners, and keep the process organised and humane.

Price range: 45,000 € - 95,000 €

Armenia

Nova Familie also operates in Armenia. We support both local and international intended parents who wish to work within the Armenian framework for surrogacy and IVF.

Our role is to help you understand your options, connect with trusted partners, and keep the process organised, transparent, and humane.

Price range: 65,000 € - 119,000 €
